About LabX

LabX is a showcase platform for AI-powered creative experiments. It serves as a unified gallery where you can discover and experience innovative AI product prototypes and creative ideas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is LabX a complete bill-splitting app?
A: No. LabX is a creative experiment showcase platform. The bill recognition feature is a demo that demonstrates AI capabilities, not a production-ready service.
Q: Why do I need to verify my email?
A: Email verification helps us ensure secure access to the app. We send a one-time verification code to your email for authentication.
Q: Why does the app need camera/photo access?
A: Camera and photo access are only used for the bill recognition demo. Images are processed locally or temporarily for AI analysis and are not stored permanently.
Q: Why does the app need contacts access?
A: Contacts access is optional and only used to help you quickly add participants in the bill-splitting demo. Contact data is used locally and not uploaded to our servers.
Q: Will there be more experiments?
A: Yes! LabX is designed to continuously showcase new AI creative experiments. New demos will be added through over-the-air updates.
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: You can delete your account from the Profile tab > Delete Account. Alternatively, contact us at the email below.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Verification code not received: Check your spam folder. Make sure you entered the correct email address.
  • Bill recognition not working: Ensure the image is clear and well-lit. Try taking a new photo with better lighting.
  • App crashes: Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, reinstall the app.
