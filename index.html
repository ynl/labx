LabX Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

LabX ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email Address: We collect your company email address for authentication purposes. This is used to verify your identity and provide access to the app.

Usage Data

  • App Usage: We collect anonymous usage data to improve our services, including features accessed and app performance metrics.
  • Error Reports: We use Sentry to collect crash reports and error logs to improve app stability.

Device Information

  • Device Identifiers: We collect device identifiers to enable push notifications and prevent fraud.
  • Push Tokens: If you enable notifications, we store your push notification token to send you updates.

Contacts (Optional)

  • Contacts Access: When you use the bill splitting feature, you may choose to import contacts from your device. This data is only used locally to help you add participants and is not uploaded to our servers.

Camera & Photos (Optional)

  • Camera/Photos Access: When you use the bill scanning feature, you may take or select photos of receipts. These images are processed to extract bill information and are not stored on our servers after processing.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and verify your identity
  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To send push notifications (with your consent)
  • To improve and optimize our app
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

Data Storage and Security

  • Your authentication data is securely stored using industry-standard encryption
  • We use Supabase for backend services with enterprise-grade security
  • Session tokens are stored securely on your device

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database services
  • Expo: App development and push notifications
  • Sentry: Error monitoring and crash reporting
  • PostHog: Anonymous analytics (opt-out available)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Disable push notifications at any time

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • You may request account deletion by contacting us
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained for up to 2 years

Children's Privacy

LabX is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: yilin@tencent.com

简介

LabX（以下简称"我们"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们在您使用我们的移动应用程序时如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

我们收集的信息

账户信息

  • 电子邮箱：我们收集您的公司电子邮箱地址用于身份验证。这用于验证您的身份并提供应用访问权限。

使用数据

  • 应用使用情况：我们收集匿名使用数据以改进我们的服务，包括访问的功能和应用性能指标。
  • 错误报告：我们使用 Sentry 收集崩溃报告和错误日志以提高应用稳定性。

设备信息

  • 设备标识符：我们收集设备标识符以启用推送通知和防止欺诈。
  • 推送令牌：如果您启用通知，我们会存储您的推送通知令牌以向您发送更新。

通讯录（可选）

  • 通讯录访问：当您使用 AA 分账功能时，您可以选择从设备导入联系人。此数据仅在本地使用以帮助您添加参与者，不会上传到我们的服务器。

相机和相册（可选）

  • 相机/相册访问：当您使用账单扫描功能时，您可以拍摄或选择收据照片。这些图片经过处理以提取账单信息，处理后不会存储在我们的服务器上。

我们如何使用您的信息

  • 验证您的身份
  • 提供和维护我们的服务
  • 发送推送通知（经您同意）
  • 改进和优化我们的应用
  • 检测和预防技术问题

数据存储和安全

  • 您的认证数据使用行业标准加密安全存储
  • 我们使用 Supabase 提供企业级安全的后端服务
  • 会话令牌安全地存储在您的设备上

第三方服务

我们使用以下第三方服务：

  • Supabase：认证和数据库服务
  • Expo：应用开发和推送通知
  • Sentry：错误监控和崩溃报告
  • PostHog：匿名分析（可选择退出）

您的权利

您有权：

  • 访问您的个人数据
  • 请求删除您的账户和数据
  • 选择退出分析跟踪
  • 随时禁用推送通知

数据保留

  • 账户数据在您的账户处于活动状态期间保留
  • 您可以通过联系我们请求删除账户
  • 匿名分析数据可能保留最多 2 年

儿童隐私

LabX 不面向 13 岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。我们将通过在此页面上发布新的隐私政策来通知您任何变更。
